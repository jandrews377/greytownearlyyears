At Early Years, we believe in the power of play, relationships, and belonging. Nestled in the heart of our Greytown community, our early childhood centre offers a nurturing environment where tamariki can explore, create, and grow in their own time.

Our experienced kaiako build strong relationships with children and whānau. We value each child's unique identity and whakapapa, and our learning environment reflects our community's diversity. Through local waiata and daily karakia kai, we embrace a bicultural approach grounded in Te Whāriki.

Transitions — particularly the important step to school — are thoughtfully supported, ensuring every child leaves us feeling confident and connected. Our values of trust, happiness, communication, motivation, and respect shape all that we do.

At Early Years, your child's journey is nurtured and celebrated — every step of the way.

Our Philosophy

Environment

Relationships

Life Long Learners

We encourage curiosity and creativity, embracing challenges and guiding learning through play while sharing and growing our sense of wonder and joy.
