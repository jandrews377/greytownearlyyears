Our Policies & Procedures

Transparent, child-centered policies that ensure every tamariki receives the highest quality care and education. Our comprehensive guidelines reflect our commitment to safety, inclusion, and best practices in early childhood development.

Child Protection Policy

This policy details our commitment to child protection and safeguarding. It includes our procedures for identifying and responding to concerns about child welfare, staff training requirements, and our zero-tolerance approach to any form of child abuse or neglect.

Enrolment Policy

This policy outlines a straightforward process to welcome new tamariki and their whānau to Early Years. We prioritise open communication, ensuring families understand our centre's philosophy, expectations, and the steps to secure a place. We value diversity and strive to create an inclusive community.

Settling in and Transitioning Policy

We understand that starting or moving between early childhood settings is a big step for tamariki and whānau. This policy focuses on gentle, individualised approaches to support children's emotional wellbeing, building trusting relationships, and fostering confidence through consistent routines and strong communication with families.

Parent Fees Policy

This policy details the payment structure and due dates to support transparent and timely fee collection. We offer clear information on fee amounts, invoicing, and any applicable subsidies or assistance. Our aim is to work collaboratively with families to ensure enrolment remains accessible and sustainable.
