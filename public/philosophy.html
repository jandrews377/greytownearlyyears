Philosophy

Our guiding principles and values that shape our early childhood education approach.

Environment

Greytown Early Years, an engaging, welcoming, safe and secure home away from home. A responsive, dynamic environment that reflects our diverse learning community and nurtures a connection with our natural world, through the concept of kaitiakitanga.

Relationships

We nurture trust, and take pride in building kind, genuine connections between our learners, their whānau and our community. We recognise and value our unique bicultural heritage by including local iwi reo and tikanga, and celebrate our diverse backgrounds and identities.

Life long learners

We encourage and support our community of learners to be curious and creative. To embrace challenge and guide their own learning through play. To share and grow their sense of wonder and joy as we investigate ideas, interests and passions together.
